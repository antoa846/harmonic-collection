Memory and Nostalgia

Harvest Moon by Neil Young

Sarah

“I think it is the most iconic because I have known it since I was young and my dad plays it on guitar for me. Plus the way he uses so many instruments in the song is sooo good. It's about growing in love with someone and how that is rare (like a harvest moon) but it's beautiful when it happens even if only for a little bit of time and it can happen again.”

Something About Us by Daft Punk

Mahani

“Star crossed lovers, LITERALLY! This romantic tune with a sci-fi backdrop acts as both noble goodbye and (final) confession. I grew up on Daft Punk, this may as well have been my first love song.”

En Nuit by VideoClub

Aiden

“En Nuit is a French song that feels so nostalgic, and is about overcoming existential boredom while finding solace in the company of someone you love.”

(They Long to Be) Close to you by The Carpenters

Ellis

“This song is meaningful to me because when I was born this song was playing in the hospital and ever since, my parents have sung it to me. Reminds me of childhood and their love for me”
